# GTK Demo

Gtk's visual identity is a colorful cube. The project needed a demo video for
`Gtk Widget Factory`. The animation is a bunch of cubes following a very pointy
path. More satisfying to watch than expected. Eevee enabled to even consider 
animation on my lousy hardware.
