# Ingenuity

NASA deserves huge credit for [releasing the Ingenuity 3D assets](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/mars-ingenuity-helicopter-3d-model/) to the public. Using those models I created two animated music videos — [Ingenuity FPV](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AxYo_qJi2XQ) and [Ingenuity's One Year of Mars Flights](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s7NoLm4xUB4) — pairing Blender renders with my own electronic music tracks.
